Mental health concerns are rising: Suicide consideration increased from 11.6% to 23.1% (2015-2019)

Key Statistics Overview

These statistics represent real challenges faced by Filipino adolescents today.

Mental Health Crisis

Suicide attempts increased from 16.8% to 24.3%

23.1% of 13-17 year-olds seriously considered suicide in 2019

Risky Behaviors

Smoking: 41% boys, 9% girls
Alcohol: 55% boys, 23% girls
Drug use: 5% boys, 0.3% girls

Malnutrition Issues

1 in 10 Filipino adolescents are overweight

Poor health-seeking behavior increases vulnerability to diseases

Menstrual Health & Hygiene

Many adolescent girls lack access to proper menstrual products and education

Menstrual health is essential for overall well-being and school attendance

Substance Use & Alcohol

Alcohol consumption is significantly higher among boys

Early substance use can lead to long-term health consequences

Violence Exposure

Many adolescents experience various forms of violence

This impacts mental health and overall well-being

Take Action

Addressing these issues requires comprehensive strategies to promote adolescent health and well-being.
