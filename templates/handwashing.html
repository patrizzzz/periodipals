Key Tips

Scrub for at least 20 seconds - sing "Happy Birthday" twice!

Don't forget between fingers, thumbs, and under nails.

Wash before eating, after restroom, and when visibly dirty.

Wet Your Hands

Turn on the tap and wet your hands with clean, running water. The temperature doesn't matter - use what's comfortable.
