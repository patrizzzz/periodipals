What is Puberty?

Puberty is a natural transformation that happens to everyone! As your body starts changing from a child's to an adult's - you're becoming the amazing person you're meant to be.

Fun Fact!

Did you know that puberty usually starts between ages 8-13 for girls? Every person is different, and each one's puberty is at a perfect time!
